## üéØ What is Customer Churn Prediction?

Customer churn prediction is the process of analyzing customer data to determine why they leave a company. This helps businesses identify at-risk customers and develop strategies to retain them. Our project covers various aspects of this process, from data preparation to machine learning and visualization using Power BI.

- **Risk Segmentation**: Identify different categories of risk among customers.
- **Business Dashboard**: A user-friendly dashboard in Power BI to visualize results.
